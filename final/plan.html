Site Plan

Site Name: The Study Guide to English Vocabulary

This name represents the core purpose and functionality of the site, which is to help teach and learn English vocabulary.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of The Study Guide to English Vocabulary is to provide an interface with which the user can request information about a specific word, such as definition, parts of speech, synonyms, and antonyms, and an interface to streamline vocabulary studies.

Scenarios:

I have an upcoming English exam. How will this site help me study?

The site will provide the user with the opportunity to create and store a list of selected words that can then be rendered in a flash card style presentation as a study guide.

My professor says that I repeat the same adjectives and verbs too much when I write. What can I do to be more diverse?

In addition to providing the definition of the word, the dicitonary search also has the option to display synonyms and antonyms to spice up your writing and keep your readers engaged.
